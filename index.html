Message from AL ASRA

A fragrance plays a very important part in shaping a person’s identity. It has the capacity to almost assume its own persona.

Born with the passion to introduce perfumery to discerning perfume lovers in a whole new shopping experience. AL ASRA Perfumes was established in the year 2015 to cater to those who enjoy perfumery as a part of their lifestyle with an exclusive range of perfumes with a strong inclination towards the masstige segment of the market.

With the first store opening in Dubai, AL ASRA Perfumes developed a genuine passion for delighting customers with expert perfume knowledge and the highest customer service standards, which remain at the forefront of everything the brand stands for today.

AL ASRA is one of the few manufacturers to introduce differentiated masstige & prestige variants mix of a few fragrances of international standards.

Today, AL ASRA Perfumes is looking to penetrate the Indian market with its presence in different verticals.

Why Now?

The beauty segment in India is at a constant double digit growth and so is the luxury segment. This makes India a prime market for fragrances.

Its growing middle class, along with its aspiring upper middle class, with the extra disposable income and broad exposure through digital avenues, is keen to indulge in premimum and luxury products and services

We, at AL ASRA believe that we can provide the experience & ambience of luxury retail environment combined with a selection of fragrances of international standard.
